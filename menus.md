## Module Assignments

### Customer Management
- **Operations**: Customer operations and order management
- **Master Data**: Customer records, order data
- **Reporting**: Customer-related reports
- **Configuration**: Customer settings

### Receiving Management
- **Operations**: Receiving operations modules
- **Master Data**: Receiving-related data
- **Reporting**: Receiving reports
- **Configuration**: Receiving settings

### Storage Management
- **Dashboard**: Performance Dashboard
- **Operations**: Quality Control, Labor Management
- **Master Data**: Inventory Age Analysis
- **Reporting**: Safety Management, Energy Management, Performance Management
- **Configuration**: Document Management, settings

### Picking & Packing
- **Operations**: Packing Check
- **Master Data**: Picking-related data
- **Reporting**: Picking reports
- **Configuration**: Picking configuration settings

### Value Added Services
- **Operations**: VAS operations
- **Master Data**: VAS data
- **Reporting**: VAS reports
- **Configuration**: VAS settings

### Shipping & Transportation
- **Operations**: Shipping operations
- **Master Data**: Shipping data
- **Reporting**: Shipping reports
- **Configuration**: Shipping settings

### Finance Management
- **Operations**: Finance operations
- **Master Data**: Billing data
- **Reporting**: Finance reports
- **Configuration**: Owner Management, settings

### Integrations
- **Operations**: Integration operations
- **Master Data**: E-commerce, systems
- **Reporting**: Integration reports
- **Configuration**: Integration settings
